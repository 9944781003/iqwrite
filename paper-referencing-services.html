Paper Referencing Formats 👇

APA (American Psychological Association) research paper format

Kumar, A.A.. (2021, May). Big Data Analytics for Edge Computing. International Journal of Artificial Intelligence, Volume 2(1), 303.-309

MLA (Modern Language Association) format

Kumar, Siva. “Big Data Analytics for Edge Computing.” International Journal of Artificial Intelligence .2021.2(1),303-309

The Chicago style of citing

Chan, Dan. The art of pandas. Panda Magazine, Nov 10, 1985.

ASA (American Sociological Association) research paper format

Feekins, Bo. 2008. “Chasing Tree Frogs.” National Geographic #182. 6-10

Oxford style

Neville, C, The complete guide to referencing and avoiding plagiarism, 2nd edn, Open University Press, New York, 2010

Harvard Referencing Style

Bloom, H. (2005) Novelists and novels. Philadelphia: Chelsea House Publishers

Vancouver style reference

Hofmeyr GJ, Neilson JP, Alfirevic Z, Crowther CA, Gulmezoglu AM, Hodnett ED et al. A Cochrane pocketbook: Pregnancy and childbirth. Chichester (GB): John Wiley & Sons; 2008

IEEE Reference Style

F. Yan, Y. Gu, Y. Wang, C. M. Wang, X. Y. Hu, H. X. Peng, et al., "Study on the interaction mechanism between laser and rock during perforation," Optics and Laser Technology, vol. 54, pp. 303-308, Dec 2013
